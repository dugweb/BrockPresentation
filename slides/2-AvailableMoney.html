How Much Money is Available To You?

  • Keep an honest record of what your personal spend in the year leading up to the sale.
  • Know your fixed verses your variable spending.
  • Assess the tax impact of how you will derive income post sale.
  • Understand the difference between your essential spending and the discretionary spending for a “better life”.
  • Know the difference between consumption and investment post-sale.
  • Determine your own “perpetuity payment”.
